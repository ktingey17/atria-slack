## CRE Terms We Use Frequently

- **NNN** = Triple net (tenant pays taxes, insurance, CAM)
- **CAM** = Common area maintenance
- **LOI** = Letter of intent
- **PSA** = Purchase and sale agreement
- **T12** = Trailing twelve months (operating statement)
- **Rent roll** = Current tenant roster with lease terms and rents
- **Proforma** = Forward-looking financial projection
- **Cap rate** = NOI / purchase price
- **Going-in cap** = Cap rate at acquisition
- **Exit cap** = Assumed cap rate at sale
- **Value-add** = Strategy involving active improvement to increase value
- **Mark-to-market** = Raising below-market rents to current market levels
- **LIHTC** = Low-Income Housing Tax Credit
- **C2P** = Construction-to-permanent (loan structure)
- **Cost seg** = Cost segregation study (tax strategy)
- **1031** = Tax-deferred exchange under IRC §1031
- **REPS** = Real Estate Professional Status (tax designation)
